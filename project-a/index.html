[ Variachroma ]

Scientific Name:

Aethereus Varians

Discovered by / at:

Generated and discovered by the observers themselves, it (they) holds differences in different worlds.

Family:

One initial mother creature; subsequent generations are created by observers.

Habitat:

A universe-based territory with stars in the background.

Appearance:

Central glowing core with halos; mother creature has pulsating vein-like extensions.

Key Characteristics:

  • 1.Visual diversity (random size, color, and motion)
  • 2.Individual personalities (warm or cool)
  • 3.Lifecycle with aging and generational renewal

What we know so far about [ Variachroma ]? This is an energy life form, it grows, dies, reproduces in the orders of the observers. In a conventional environment without orders, it floats and breathes. Its descendants sometimes differ from others in appearance and “personalities”.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Mouse-based and “space” keyboard interactions. While the creature and its descendants hold differences in their “personalities”, the creatures will show different responses to the observers’ interactions. Try to move your mouse to find their characteristics.
